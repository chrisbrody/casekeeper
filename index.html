CaseKeeper Helps You Win Your Case
 

Attorney Accountability

Understanding what's happening with your case is important, now you have a way to follow it more closely than ever before. Work with your attorney and ensure they're holding up their end of the bargain.

Simply Organized

All of your attorney discussions, notes, and files for your case are all in one neatly organized place. Access it from anywhere on the planet, anytime you want. Make changes to the case as much as you want.

Clear Communication

You and your attorney have lives outside of your case, which makes it hard for both of you to be available at the same time. Now, you have a way to chat when you're available, without disrupting your schedules.

Why We Built CaseKeeper
 
Sam, one of our founders, went through a child custody case that tooks years to resolve. He worked with 3 different lawyers, and had been to court dozens of times without a solid resolution, frequently because of mistakes made by his lawyer at the time. He couldn't take it, and resolved to figure out a solution. After working out exactly what he needed, he decided that the best option was to build a program that would give him back control of his case. Sam finished the program, and found it so useful, that he dropped his web design business to bring CaseKeeper to the public.
Current Features
 

Have Discussions

Drop the flooded inbox and start using discussions to keep all of your attorney communication in one organized location.

share Notes

Tired of tearing up your house for that one note you need right now? Start saving and sharing notes with your lawyer in one place.

Upload Files

Upload files, documents, forms, images, audio, and even videos, to your case page, to keep it on the straight and narrow.

Download Files

Download a file when you need it, from anywhere. You can even download files that your attorney uploads, and vice-versa.

Email Alerts

Every time your attorney adds something to your case page, you'll receive an email alert letting you know the instant it happens.

Archive Case

When your case if finished, archive it so you'll always have a record of it. Open it back up whenever you like and get what you need.
